When I say a multi-day battle, I don't mean something absolutely huge like Operation Barbarossa. No tabletop game is going to simulate a battle lasting many months with hundreds of divisions on both sides. There are many excellent board game type wargames and computer based wargames that simulate massive battles very well, but that is outside the scope of this post.

This post is intended to outline your options for playing corps or at a push army level multi-day battles in a relatively short time of a few hours.

## Time Constraints
Every set of rules says that you can complete the game in a few hours at your local wargaming club. Some games will allow you to do that, but in my experience those rules are few and far between for large scale battles.

## Rules Options
- Eisenhower
- Rommel 
- Sitz Krieg on Hatz
- Division Commander
