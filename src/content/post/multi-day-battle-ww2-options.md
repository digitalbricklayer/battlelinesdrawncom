First, what this is not about. When I say a multi-day battle, I don't mean something absolutely huge like Operation Barbarossa. No tabletop game is going to simulate a battle lasting many months with hundreds of divisions on both sides. There are many excellent board game type wargames that simulate massive battles very well, but that is outside the scope of this post.

This post is intended to outline your options for playing corps or at a push army level battles over a number of days. 

## Rules Options

- Eisenhower
- Rommel 
- Sitz Krieg on Hatz
